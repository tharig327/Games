HOW TO PLAY

Tap a bottle to pick up its top color, then tap another bottle to pour it in.

You can only pour onto an empty bottle or one whose top color matches. A bottle is full at 4 units.

Sort every color into its own bottle — with no mixed bottles left — to win the level.
